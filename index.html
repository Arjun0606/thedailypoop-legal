1. Introduction

Welcome to TheDailyPoop! We take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Username, date of birth, gender (via Sign in with Apple)
  • Profile Information: Profile picture (optional), custom gender (optional)
  • Poop Drops: Timestamp, location (city/state only), rating, caption, music links
  • Social Interactions: Friend requests, reactions (emojis), friend connections

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Location Data: City and state only (NOT exact coordinates). We use Apple's CoreLocation to determine your approximate location when you log a poop drop.
  • Usage Statistics: Streak count, total drops, badges earned, countries/continents visited
  • Device Information: Device token for push notifications (handled by Apple)

2.3 Third-Party Services

  • Apple Sign In: We use Apple's Sign in with Apple for authentication. Apple provides us with a unique user identifier and optionally your name.
  • CloudKit (iCloud): All your data is stored in Apple's CloudKit (iCloud) database. You must have an active iCloud account to use TheDailyPoop.
  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data for ad targeting. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Music Services: When you paste Spotify or Apple Music links, we fetch song metadata (title, artist, cover art) via their APIs. We do NOT access your music library.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Core Functionality: Log poop drops, display them on the map, track stats and streaks
  • Social Features: Connect you with friends, show friend activity, send push notifications
  • Gamification: Award badges, maintain leaderboards, track achievements
  • Push Notifications: Alert you when friends drop poops, send friend requests, react to your drops, or when you need streak reminders
  • Improve the App: Analyze usage patterns (anonymously) to improve features
  • Display Ads: Show relevant ads via Google AdMob to support free features

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 What We Share

With Friends: When you add friends, they can see:

  • Your username and profile picture
  • Your poop drops (city/state, timestamp, rating, caption, music)
  • Your stats and badges
  • Your position on the leaderboard

4.2 What We DON'T Share

  • Exact Location: We NEVER share your exact GPS coordinates. Only city/state is visible.
  • Personal Data: We NEVER sell your personal information to third parties.
  • Private Data: Your Apple ID, email, phone number are NEVER shared with other users.

4.3 Third-Party Services

  • Apple (CloudKit): All data is stored in CloudKit. Apple has access to this data as per their Privacy Policy.
  • Google (AdMob): AdMob collects device identifiers for ad serving. See Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • Encrypted Storage: All data is encrypted in CloudKit (managed by Apple)
  • Secure Authentication: Sign in with Apple provides industry-standard security
  • Push Notifications: Delivered via Apple's APNs (Apple Push Notification service), which uses end-to-end encryption
  • No Passwords: We don't store passwords. Authentication is handled by Apple.
